Challenges I Overcame

Week 1-2

CSS Architecture

Learning to organize CSS with the 7-1 pattern felt overwhelming at first. But once I understood the importance of maintainable code, everything made sense. This changed how I approach every project now.

Week 3-4

Sass & Advanced Selectors

Variables, mixins, and nesting in Sass felt like learning a new language. The power of @extend and functions initially confused me, but practicing with real projects helped me understand when and how to use each feature.

Week 5-6

Responsive Design

Making layouts truly responsive across all devices was harder than expected. Media queries, mobile-first approach, and managing breakpoints required constant testing and refinement.

Week 7-8

CSS Grid & Animations

Grid revolutionized my layout skills, and creating smooth, performant animations taught me patience and attention to detail. These became my favorite topics in the entire course.
